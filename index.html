About

David is a UX designer with an emphasis on social impact. For more than 15 years David has worked in local, regional and international organizations. His professional goal is to design products that bring value to the end user and the community.

During the design process, David is a concise written communicator, effective project manager, and cross functional collaborator. He is also an attentive and insightful listener that anticipates the needs of the customer, and synthesizes large amounts of information into simple concepts.

David specializes in UX research, UX design and front-end development.
